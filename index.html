Frequently asked questions

What are “anti‑cheating signals”?

They’re behavioral and technical indicators like copy/paste bursts, unusual window focus, timing anomalies, and language‑style shifts. They don’t auto‑fail anyone; they help interviewers interpret results fairly.

Do candidates know what’s being captured?

Yes. We show a clear in‑product notice describing the signals collected and the purpose. Candidates can proceed with informed consent.

How is AI usage measured?

When the built‑in assistant is enabled, we log prompts, responses, and how candidates adapt the output. Interviewers see a transparent usage summary alongside the scorecard.

Is external AI use allowed?

You choose the policy per interview. Using the embedded assistant keeps usage auditable; banning external tools is optional and configurable.

What about privacy?

We minimize personal data and collect only the signals needed for fairness and auditability. Organization admins can set retention windows and export data when required.
